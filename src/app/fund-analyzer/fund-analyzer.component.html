What is Fund Analyzer?

The Fund Analyzer automatically applies a fund’s applicable fees and commonly available discounts, The Fund Analyzer helps you sort through and compare more than 30,000 mutual funds, exchange-traded funds, exchange-traded notes and money market funds, The Fund Analyzer does the math for you and shows how fees and expenses impact the value of your fund over time.

Design Task

In the project, our team were responsible for redesigning the Fund Analyzer with a more intuitive and user friendly UI. In this practice, we tried to adopt Microsoft’s newly released fluent design for its simplicity and mordern look and feel.

Search Result View

Under such artistic approach, we minimize the use of decorative line and color background, and put primary focus on spacing and treatment on elements interactivity to make information stands out.
